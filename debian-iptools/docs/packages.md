✅ What’s Included (high level)

Core: iproute2, net-tools, ethtool, arping, mtr-tiny, traceroute
DNS: dnsutils (dig, nslookup), ldnsutils (drill)
HTTP/TLS: curl, wget, httpie, openssl, ca-certificates
Bandwidth: iperf3, nuttcp, bwping
Sockets/Ports: netcat-openbsd, socat, ncat
Capture/Analysis: tcpdump, tshark, ngrep
Firewall/NAT: iptables, nftables, conntrack
Monitoring: iftop, nethogs, bmon, vnstat, procps
Security/Scanning: nmap, hping3, arp-scan
General: jq, rsync, openssh-client, whois, less
